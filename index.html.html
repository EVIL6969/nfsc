Have Questions?

His multi-specialty training allows him to approach each patient's aesthetic goals with a comprehensive understanding of facial anatomy, skin science, and dental structure — a combination that is extremely rare.
A typical FUE hair transplant session takes 6–8 hours depending on the number of grafts needed. Initial hair shedding (shock loss) is normal and occurs within 2–4 weeks. New hair growth begins around 3–4 months, with full, natural results typically visible at 12–15 months post-procedure.
Procedures are performed under appropriate anesthesia, ensuring you experience no pain during the surgery. Recovery varies by procedure — minor treatments may require just a few days, while surgeries like rhinoplasty or facelift may have a recovery period of 2–3 weeks. Dr. Nikhil will discuss your specific recovery plan in detail during consultation.
The number of sessions varies based on the skin concern, its severity, and your skin's response to treatment. Generally, most conditions like acne or pigmentation show significant improvement after 4–8 sessions. A personalized treatment schedule will be outlined during your skin consultation at NFSC.
Digital Smile Designing (DSD) is a planning tool that uses photos and digital software to visualize your new smile before any dental work begins. Dr. Nikhil uses DSD to show you an accurate preview of proposed veneers, whitening, or alignment changes, allowing you to co-design your ideal smile with full transparency.
